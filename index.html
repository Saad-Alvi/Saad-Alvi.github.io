Personal Ideas

Overview

This is a report showing how Austcorp or any other construction company should start taking step towards becoming more eco-friendly. The best and newest way for construction companies to start
taking these steps is by using eco-friendly material also known as Sustainable Building. This is done by using natural material such as wood. Using technology and skills we have learnt over the
years we can build a cost effect ad ecofriendly building. This report shows us the basics on how we can move towards becoming more ecofriendly

Motivation

One of the biggest issue in Construction currently is Eco friendly construction material (Sustainable Building). The ASEAN region is set to grow 5.4% each year for the next 10 years which is
equivalent to the fifth largest economy. Saying this Australia will construct more than 40 million square meters of certified green building space by 2020. Saying this Austcorp Pty Ltd should
follow. Because of this it will give us many benefits such as good face and also the main problem of helping the environment. Every year the Laws on construction is getting tougher specially
on Sustainable Building, If we take an early step towards this it will be more convenient for us to adjust to future Compliance changes.

Description

What is sustainable building? Sustainable building can be categorised in some simple ways such as helping keep and protecting the environmental resources that surround the job site. Minimising
pollution degradation and trash while we are constructing. Using energy to the best of its ability. Protecting the community around the job site. We can also see a longer life expectancy in houses
using these materials also the owners can have a more cost effect renovation since we are using natural materials. General utility will be cheaper such as gas, oil and even water. While helping the
environment and society around it. Also at the end of the building life expectancy we can recycle all the material used in the building. While this is still fairly new to the industry standards around
the world are slowly pushing towards having it. This will also allow the company to help standards move forward towards these goals. These Points can be seen by a project done in Norway. The Treet (The Tree) project has shown the world how sustainable building is not helping the environment but also cost effective. This is a high-rise
building which made mostly by wood by using prefabrication materials. The recognition of building the world’s largest high rise wood building gave the company a big boost and now has changed the world of
construction. Also the company who made this building was awarded many different awards some being. Wood construction of the year and prefab house of the year. This has made this company an eco-friendly
household name in the construction industry Austcorp can slowly integrate this into their future building plans by starting off slow. This would not only change the way Australia portrays the company but would have a world-wide recognition. Also
allowing the Customers to be happy because of a more affordable way of living. By building house from Eco friendly materials we allow them to not only be able have less renovations but also more affordable
renovations. Also having features such as internal heating systems connected to a larger heating system within the house can hold back on individual housing energy. Our eco-friendly buildings will also leave
a very meniscal carbon footprint not only in the building cycle but if the whole houses life expectancy because we will be able to recycle most of the materials used in the building. Being more cost effect for
Austcorp will save us money time and labour which are the three main expensive the company will have. Also coming to the fact that laws in local and international industry are changing to support these
Austcorp will not only ahead of its competition but ready for any changes made by the government.

Tools and Technologies

Best thing about Sustainable building is that all resources needed are natural such as using wooden beams to construct a house or a building in the past we didn’t have the technology to calculate how we can
use wood and concert slabs to stabilise and make the living conditions possible. By using softwares such as Arcarhicad we can make a 3D visuals of the houses we are going to make and see if it is possible for it
to be stable and liveable. Because of softwares like these we are able to see many outcomes in the industry

Skill Required

Austcorp already has most of materials needed. The company has softwares that are used to design houses. Also the company already uses all the materials needed such as glass, wood and concert. All the company needs
would need to do is understand how to calculate the best way to build houses using these materials only. Using projects like the Treet and other similar projects we can learn the exact resources necessary to build
what the company needs.

Outcome

Well the most important outcome that will come from this will be decreasing our carbon footprint helping the environment and the society the building is in. Also the recognition Austcorp will get by taking the step
forward in construction will boost the company’s image. Furthermore helping give customers a more affordable lifestyle can really change Australian economy and how the construction industry is seen in Australia. This
improvement can really help the company make the right steps to takes Austcorp to the next level.
